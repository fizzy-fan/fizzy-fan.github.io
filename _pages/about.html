The THRIVIN’ Group is an interdisciplinary research lab based at the University of Alberta, housed within the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering. We focus on advancing the science of transportation resilience and public health under climate stress, combining engineering, data science, and environmental health.

By bridging civil engineering, climate resilience, and data science, the THRIVIN’ Group is committed to building tools and knowledge that help communities adapt to a rapidly changing world.
